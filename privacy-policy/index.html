Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

January 2, 2026

At NOIRI Studio, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have regarding your information.

Information We Collect

We only collect personal information that you voluntarily provide through our contact form, including:

  • Name

  • Email address

  • Message or inquiry details

How We Use Your Information

We use your information strictly for:

  • Responding to your inquiries

  • Communicating regarding potential collaborations or services

  • Improving our communication and client experience

Your information is not shared, sold, or distributed to third parties, unless required by law.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no digital transmission is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the information we have about you

  • Ask for corrections or deletion of your data

  • Withdraw consent at any time by contacting us

To exercise any of these rights, contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
