The story behind it

Game in Motion celebrates the movement, strategy, and emotion inherent in basketball. Each black-and-white frame highlights the athleticism and focus of the players, freezing a split-second of action that tells the story of the game. From a fast break to a crucial jump shot, the images capture both skill and tension in the courtâs atmosphere.
